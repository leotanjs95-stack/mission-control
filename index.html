How to use this control center

This board is a status mirror — it shows what your agents are doing. It refreshes itself; you don't run it.

⚡ AUTO agents — Gem & Nova

Run by themselves in the cloud on a schedule (GitHub Actions). They work even with your Mac off and you logged out. You do nothing. Gem sends Meta reports weekly; Nova processes lead scrapes every 5 minutes.

✋ ON-DEMAND agents — PJ & Cher

Run when you ask Claude. Open Claude Code and say e.g. "PJ, make a 9:16 ad video for …" or "Cher, pull this week's Google Ads report." Creative + one-off tasks need a fresh instruction each time.

Numbers and the activity feed update automatically every 15 minutes (and this page re-checks every 2 minutes). Share this link with the team — no login needed to view.
